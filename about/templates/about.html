About Us

Responsible Travel

We make sure your money stays in the country. By working with local, family-run restaurants, we contribute to the local economy by making small businesses succeed. By exploring the city by foot, you get to see more! You also get to offset your carbon footprint, which is always ace.

(Mostly) Cruelty Free

We believe in a business model which is fair to all species on this planet. We reject animal or human exploitation and we do our absolute best to operate outside of trade based on violence. While we understand we cannot avoid all situations in which sentient beings may suffer directly or indirectly, we try to be as congruent as possible.

Foodies By Nature

We are people who love to eat, and eat well. Our passion for authentic and creative flavours which reflect the local culture goes from traditional pre hispanic foods to modern fusion cuisine. Our staff can’t wait to feed you!

Not Just Food

Yes, who doesn’t love to explore a city and eat at the same time? However, our tours are not only about food. We thrive to show you off-the-beaten path neighborhoods and give you a true feel of the city’s history and contemporary context.
